--- layout: archive title: "Publications & Manuscripts" permalink: /publications/ author_profile: true --- {% include base_path %} This page lists my selected manuscripts, competition papers, and research project reports. Some works are project manuscripts or under preparation, and are therefore not formal peer-reviewed publications. ## Manuscripts ### Two-Stream Interactive Joint Learning of Scene Parsing and Geometric Vision Tasks Guanfeng Tang, Hongbo Zhao, Ziwei Long, **Jiayao Li,** Bohong Xiao, Wei Ye, Hanli Wang, Rui Fan Under review at IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Vehicles. arXiv preprint. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.13588) / [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.13588) This work investigates bidirectional interaction between scene parsing and geometric vision tasks, including semantic segmentation, instance segmentation, stereo matching, and optical flow. --- ### Are LLMs Reliable for Recommending Libraries and Their Usage? An Empirical Study Yuying Wang, Yilian Yu, **Jiayao Li,** Maojun Huang, et al. Under review at Internetware 2026 (CCF C). This work empirically studies the reliability of large language models in recommending software libraries and supporting code migration tasks. --- ### Can video generation replace cinematographers? Research on the cinematic language of generated video Xiaozhe Li, Kai Wu, Siyi Yang, YiZhan Qu, Guohua Zhang, Zhiyu Chen, **Jiayao Li,** Jiangchuan Mu, et al. arXiv preprint. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2412.12223) / [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2412.12223) This work studies cinematic language in text-to-video generation, focusing on shot framing, angles, and camera movements. It introduces a cinematic language dataset, CameraCLIP for alignment evaluation, and CLIPLoRA for dynamic cinematic style composition. --- ## Competition Papers ### Lunar Colony Transportation Planning and Robust Optimization **Jiayao Li**, et al. 2026 Mathematical Contest in Modeling, Problem B. **Outstanding Winner & INFORMS Award**. This paper formulates a transportation planning and robust optimization framework for lunar colony logistics under resource, scheduling, and uncertainty constraints. --- ## Project Reports ### Single-View 3D Occupancy Prediction and Spatial Perception — ViPOcc Framework Project report / research project. This project explores monocular 3D occupancy prediction by leveraging depth and semantic-instance priors from visual foundation models, inverse-depth alignment, and semantic-guided sampling strategies. --- ### Dynamic Robot Navigation with 2D LiDAR-Based Pedestrian Detection Project report. This project integrates 2D LiDAR-based pedestrian detection with reinforcement-learning-based dynamic robot navigation in ROS and Gazebo environments.
